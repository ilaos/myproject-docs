# Alma AI Chat

## Overview

Alma AI Chat is a global chat assistant built into every page of AlmaSEO. It gives you instant, conversational access to your platform data — sites, posts, automation jobs, SEO audits, and more — without leaving the page you're on.

The global chat assistant can query your live data across all your sites — including Google Analytics and Google Search Console — and maintains a full conversation history.

---

## What It Does

- **Answers questions about your sites** — list your sites, check automation status, review recent posts, diagnose failed jobs
- **Queries live data** — Alma uses 18 built-in tools to pull real information from your database, Google Analytics, and Google Search Console in real time
- **Provides SEO advice** — get actionable recommendations tailored to your business context
- **Remembers your conversation** — chat history persists across browser sessions until you clear it
- **Knows where you are** — Alma detects which site/page you're viewing and uses that as context

## How to Use It

### Opening the Chat

1. Look for the **purple robot button** in the bottom-right corner of any page (above the green notepad button)
2. Click it to open the chat panel
3. On first open, you'll see a welcome screen with suggested prompts

### Asking Questions

Type your question and press **Enter** to send. Examples:

- "What sites do I have?"
- "How is my automation performing?"
- "Show me the last 5 posts for my site"
- "Why did my last automation job fail?"
- "What content gaps should I focus on?"
- "Give me a health check on site #3"
- "How is my site performing on Google Search Console?"
- "What keywords are driving the most traffic?"
- "Show me the device breakdown from Search Console"
- "Compare my organic traffic sources with my search performance"

Alma will automatically use the right tools to look up your data and respond with real, specific answers.

### Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Enter | Send message |
| Shift + Enter | New line |
| Escape | Close chat |

### Clearing History

Click the **trash icon** in the chat header to clear your conversation and start fresh. This also resets the message counter.

## Key Benefits

- **Always available** — accessible from any page, no need to navigate to a specific section
- **Real data, not guesses** — Alma queries your actual database rather than giving generic advice
- **Context-aware** — knows which site you're viewing and factors that into responses

## Notes

- **Message limit**: 60 messages per session. Clear the conversation to reset.
- **Input limit**: Messages are capped at 2,000 characters.
- **Privacy**: Alma only accesses sites you own. Passwords, API keys, and OAuth tokens are never included in responses.
- **Conversation trimming**: Only the last 20 messages are sent to the AI for context, keeping responses fast.
- **Timeout**: Requests time out after 60 seconds. If this happens, try a simpler or more specific question.
- **Non-SEO questions**: Alma will politely redirect you if you ask about topics outside SEO and digital marketing.
