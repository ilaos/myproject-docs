# Decision Intelligence

**Tier:** Core/Free | **Type:** Core Engine

!!! tip "Key Insight"
    Addresses a major AI writing flaw — LLMs hedge everything to avoid being wrong. AlmaSEO is trained to take a position. Marketable as "content that actually helps people decide, not content that says 'it depends.'”

## Overview

Programs the engine to be a trusted consultant, not a fence-sitter. Bans hedging phrases like "Generally speaking" or "It depends." Uses comparison focus and business data to take a definitive stand and offer verdicts. Readers get clear guidance, not wishy-washy summaries.

## Why It Matters

Programs the engine to be a trusted consultant. Content that helps people decide.

## Target Audience

Agencies, DIY Business Owners, Freelancers
