# Article Creation Form

**Tier:** Core/Free | **Type:** User-Facing UI

## Overview

The central interface where users configure all settings for a new article. Houses tone, length, language, permalink, keywords, scheduling, categories, special instructions, and image options. This is the cockpit — every decision about how an article gets written and published happens here.

## Why It Matters

The central interface/cockpit where every decision about an article happens.

## Requirements

- **Integrations:** OpenAI API, Wordpress API

## Target Audience

Agencies, DIY Business Owners, Freelancers
