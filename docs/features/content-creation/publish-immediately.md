## Overview

Toggle that controls whether the article publishes live or saves as draft in WordPress. When off, content is saved as draft for review before going live. Simple but essential for workflows where approval is needed before publishing.

## Why It Matters

Toggle for publish live vs. save as draft. Essential for approvals.
