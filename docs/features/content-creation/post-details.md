# Post Details

**Tier:** Core/Free | **Type:** User-Facing UI

!!! tip "Key Insight"
     This screen is proof that AlmaSEO doesn't stop at content generation — it follows through. The combination of Google NLP validation, Search Console submission, and IndexNow creates an end-to-end story competitors can't match.

## Overview

The post-creation hub where users view, validate, and take action on generated content. Displays the full article preview alongside metadata, quality scores, and distribution tools. This is the "command center" for a completed article — everything from reviewing SEO metadata to submitting to search engines to sharing on social media happens here.

## Why It Matters

The command center for a completed article — view, validate, and take action.

## Target Audience

Agencies, DIY Business Owners, Freelancers
