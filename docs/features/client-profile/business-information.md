# Business Information

**Tier:** Core/Free | **Type:** User-Facing UI

!!! tip "Key Insight"
    This is where E-E-A-T magic begins — and users don't even realize it. The "About Us" field is parsed at content-generation time to extract tenure years ("since 2005" → 20 years). The "Industry Type" auto-maps to authority titles (legal → Attorney, plumbing → Technician, mold remediation → Specialist). Users fill in normal business info; AlmaSEO turns it into authority signals automatically. This invisible intelligence is worth calling out in marketing: "Your business profile becomes your content's credibility.”

## Overview

Core business details that power content personalization and E-E-A-T signals. Fields include: Company Logo (PNG/JPG/JPEG/SVG, max 2MB, 400x400px recommended), Business Name, Business Owner, About Us (used for SEO and business profiles — also parsed for tenure years via regex), Contact Information (Phone, Email, Website URL), Business Hours (freeform, one day per line), and Industry Type. Physical addresses, Google Maps URLs, and service areas are managed in the Locations tab — not here.
