# Business Profile

**Tier:** Pro | **Type:** User-Facing UI

!!! tip "Key Insight"
    GBP is critical for local businesses. Ties directly into Locations and NAP workflows and can become a major agency selling point.

Google Services child tab for connecting and managing Google Business Profile (GBP) for the client’s locations.

## Why It Matters

Supports local SEO workflows by centralizing GBP access for insights, optimization, and location-aware reporting.

## Requirements

- **Integrations:** Google API

## Target Audience

Agencies, DIY Business Owners, Freelancers
