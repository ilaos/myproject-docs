# Services & Areas

**Tier:** Pro | **Type:** User-Facing UI

!!! tip "Key Insight"
    Writers automatically pull Strategic Service Focus, Strategic Location Focus, and notable achievements/offers from the Client Profile to generate targeted copy (e.g., “Serving Mount Pleasant” or “specializing in 3D virtual tours”).

## Overview

A Client Profile hub to define what services the business offers and the geographic areas it targets, including focus services and priority target areas. This information is reused by the article writer for accurate, location- and service-specific copy.

## Why It Matters

Keeps service offerings, target locations, and notable achievements in one place so content can stay aligned with business priorities and include the right services and areas in each article.

## Requirements

- **Integrations:** OpenAI API

## Target Audience

Agencies, DIY Business Owners, Freelancers
